<Accordion title="Can myIDFI AI handle multiple languages?">
  Yes, myIDFI AI supports multiple languages, making it an ideal solution for
  global businesses looking to engage with users in different regions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What types of businesses can use myIDFI AI?">
myIDFI AI is built to automate customer support, so it doesn’t matter what type of business you have or what services you offer. myIDFI AI is ideal for businesses of all sizes, including startups, SMBs, enterprises, and government agencies.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does myIDFI AI fully replace my support team?">
No, it doesn’t. We believe the human touch is essential for great customer service, which is why we built the myIDFI AI platform to work alongside your support team. myIDFI AI handles simple and repetitive queries automatically, allowing your team to focus on personalized support for complex issues, even with limited human resources.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title=" I already have a support team. Why should I consider myIDFI AI?">
  myIDFI AI doesn’t replace your team. It supports them. It automates repetitive tasks, allowing your team to focus on complex issues and VIP customers while reducing overall response times and improving the customer experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title=" Is this only for large companies? ">
  Not at all. myIDFI AI is designed to make enterprise-grade customer support accessible to businesses of any size. Many small businesses see huge benefits from myIDFI AI's ability to automate support without large support teams at a lower cost
</Accordion>
